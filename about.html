Who am I?

I'm a 19 year old computer science student based in Krems, Austria. I was born in Chisinau, Moldova. My first programming experience was basic C# console applications, then found out about a game hacking forum called UnknownCheats, which inspired me to make my own internal DLL CS:GO cheat built in C++ and with Windows apis. After CS2 released and my cheats weren't compatible anymore I started fiddling with OpenGL and Emulator Development, which I still enjoy doing, even tough I am not that good at them.
